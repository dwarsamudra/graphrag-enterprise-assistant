Company HR Policy Document

1. Leave Policy:
- Employees are entitled to 20 paid leaves per year.
- Sick leave: 10 days annually.
- Casual leave: 10 days annually.
- Unused leaves cannot be carried forward.

2. Work From Home Policy:
- Employees can work from home up to 2 days per week.
- Manager approval is required.

3. Onboarding Process:
- New employees must complete documentation within 3 days.
- Mandatory training is required in the first week.

4. Performance Evaluation:
- Employees are evaluated quarterly.
- KPI-based performance system is used.

5. Code of Conduct:
- Professional behavior is mandatory.
- Any misconduct may lead to termination.
